# tes3markData

A simple structure to hold mark/recall data.

## Properties

### `cell`

*Read-only*. The cell to recall to.

**Returns**:

* `result` ([tes3cell](../../types/tes3cell))

***

### `position`

The position to recall to.

**Returns**:

* `result` ([tes3vector3](../../types/tes3vector3))

***

### `rotation`

The rotation to use when recalling.

**Returns**:

* `result` (number)

***
